Biography

Peng Wang received his B.S. degree (2018) from Huazhong Agricultural University. Now, he is a Ph.D. candidate from Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics (WNLO), Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). He is part of the Intelligent Data Storage and Management Laboratory (IDSM), advised by Prof. Ke Zhou.


Research Interests

Computer System Architecture, Cloud Storage, Cache, and AI for Storage.
