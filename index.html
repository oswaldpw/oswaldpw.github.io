Biography

Peng Wang is currently a Post Doctoral Fellow working with Prof. Xiangshui Miao and Prof. Ke Zhou at Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics of Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Now, He is a member of the Intelligent Data Storage and Management Laboratory (IDSM). Before that, he received his Ph.D. degree in computer system structure from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in June 2024, supervised by Prof. Ke Zhou. Peng Wang received his B.S. degree (2018) from Huazhong Agricultural University (HZAU).


Research Interests

Computer System Architecture, Cloud Storage, Cache, and AI for Storage.
